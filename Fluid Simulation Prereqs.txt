Velocity grid
Position, x
Time, t
Density, P (constant)
scalar pressure field, p(x, t)
Kinematic viscosity, v
F = (fx, fy)


Navier-Stokes:
du/dt = -(u * delta) * v - 1/P * delta * p + v * delta^2 * u + F

Advecation:
-(u * delta) * v

Pressure:
1/P * delta * p

Diffusion:
v * delta^2

External Forces:
F
